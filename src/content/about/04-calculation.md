## The Calculation

Summa uses the following algorithm:

1. Convert Roman numerals to integers.
2. Canonicalise pounds and shillings to amounts in pence (£1 = 240d; 1/&#x2212; = 12d).
3. Sum the amounts denominated in pence.
4. Convert the (pence) sum back into its £, s, d denominations.
5. Convert each amount to Roman numerals for display.
